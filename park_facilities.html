About Brean Sands

Newmans at Unity park

If you’re looking for the place that has it all, Unity Beach at Brean Sands is the place for you, a stunning indoor and outdoor swimming pool complex, a wide choice of bars and restaurants with great entertainment for all the family. Pick from a round of golf in the morning, an afternoon fishing by the lake or a fun filled day of adventure at the largest theme park in the south west.
